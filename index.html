About

About the Project

EYSAE is designed to strengthen youth capacities in sustainable agriculture and entrepreneurship by combining learning, cooperation, practical engagement and international exchange.

Why this project matters

Young people need practical pathways to build green skills, entrepreneurial confidence and stronger links to sustainable local economies.

What EYSAE delivers

The project creates learning opportunities, cooperation activities, visibility actions and transferable results for youth workers, young participants and partner organisations.

Objectives

Project Objectives

01

Strengthen green competencies

Support young people in understanding sustainable agriculture, environmental responsibility and local resilience.

02

Promote entrepreneurship

Encourage initiative, innovation and entrepreneurial thinking linked to rural and sustainable development opportunities.

03

Build international cooperation

Create stronger collaboration between partners, communities and youth stakeholders across participating countries.

04

Increase visibility and transferability

Share methods, activities and project results in a way that supports broader use and long-term sustainability.
